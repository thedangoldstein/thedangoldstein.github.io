PBS documentary film “Broadside: Emerging Empires Collide”

I modeled and textured everything in these scenes EXCEPT the ships, boats and people.

Created numerous high polygon and low polygon 3D models.

Created numerous detailed UV and procedural textures.

Implemented and modified motion capture files for the people in these scenes.

Created realistic environments and ecosystems using Vue xStream.

Set up large scenes with animated camera moves and realistic lighting.

Animated scenes for ship battles, cityscapes, and strategic maps.

Setup and rendered out draft plus final animations.

Composited and edited multi-layered scenes in Adobe After Effects for final product.
