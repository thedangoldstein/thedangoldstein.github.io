About

 

       3D Artist, Animator, Motion Graphics Designer

          15+ Years of Professional Experience

             Lives In the Greater Boston Area

              Bachelor of Arts in Illustration

            Bachelor of Arts in Digital Media

         University of Massachusetts Dartmouth 2007

Creative at the core, I have over fifteen years of industry experience as a 3D artist, animator and motion graphics designer developing high quality visualizations for a wide variety of companies and clients seeking compelling business solutions.

 

With a lifetime passion for being a creative, I continue to be extremely dedicated to bettering myself and the company I engage with along my journey. My goal is to build and maintain positive relationships wherever I go and to help drive creative teams to new exciting heights and beyond.

 

My work has been a part of television broadcasts, commercials, independent films, medical demonstrations, marketing strategies, architectural visualizations and educational media. Other projects of mine have been used by the United States Government and for legal firms.

 

Feel free to reach out and contact me for any creative needs!
